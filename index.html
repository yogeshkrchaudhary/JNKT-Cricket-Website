About Me

Vishwajeet Gopala - Batting All-Rounder & Head Coach

I am Vishwajeet Gopala, a dedicated Batting All-Rounder with a deep-rooted passion for cricket. My journey in the sport spans over several years, during which I have had the privilege of representing Bihar at the Under 23 and Under 25 levels multiple times. My commitment to the game has also seen me participate in various high-level tournaments, including the recent Bihar Cricket League. With extensive experience on the field and a thorough understanding of the game, I am now channeling my knowledge and skills into coaching. As a Head Coach, I aim to inspire and guide the next generation of cricketers, helping them realize their full potential and achieve their dreams.
